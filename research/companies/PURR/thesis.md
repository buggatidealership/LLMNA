## The business model

Hyperliquid Strategies Inc (HSI) is a corporate shell whose entire business is:
1. Issue equity to public market (raise USD)
2. Use proceeds to acquire HYPE tokens
3. Hold HYPE on balance sheet
4. Stake some HYPE to generate yield ($2.6M staking revenue Q3 FY26)
5. Repeat

Per [SEC 8-K](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/0002078856/000119312525310714/d79611dex991.htm) and [The Block](https://www.theblock.co/post/400512/hyperliquid-strategies-net-loss): formed from Sonnet BioTherapeutics merger; primary focus per company statements is "maximize shareholder value through accumulating HYPE."

This is the same playbook as MSTR (Saylor's MicroStrategy BTC strategy). The PURR equity trades at premium/discount to the value of held HYPE tokens (NAV) based on:
- Market's HYPE price view
- Ability to raise additional capital
- Staking yield contribution
- Operating expense drag
