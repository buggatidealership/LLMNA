Targeting **$200M ARR by end of fiscal 2027** with expansion to 6,000 GPUs total in Canada per [Investing.com transcript](https://www.investing.com/news/transcripts/earnings-call-transcript-hive-digital-tech-sees-strong-q1-fy2026-growth-93CH-4289903).

**Position recommendation:** Watchlist. HPC division has clearer revenue traction than Bitfarms ($15-20M ARR vs Bitfarms still at master-planning). But $200M ARR target is 2+ years out. Lower priority than CORZ/IREN/APLD.
