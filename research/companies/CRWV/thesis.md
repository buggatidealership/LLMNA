## What CRWV Actually Is

Layer 2 GPU infrastructure in AI stack:
- Buys NVDA GPUs at scale → builds/leases datacenters + power + cooling → rents to AI labs (OpenAI, Anthropic), hyperscalers (Microsoft, Meta), enterprises (Jane Street $6B agreement April 2026)
- Multi-year contracts dominate; spot secondary
- Owns 1+ GW active power; 3.5 GW contracted; 8 GW target by 2030 per [ouranostech T3](https://www.ouranostech.com/blogs/coreweave-statistics)
- Core Scientific acquisition adds 1.3 GW
- 250K+ GPU fleet early 2025 (likely understated mid-2026)
- Liquid cooling at GB200 scale + Vera Rubin first-mover via Meta agreement
