## The business

Pure-play GPUaaS provider per [BriefGlance](https://briefglance.com/companies/sharonai-holdings-inc):
- **GPU-as-a-Service (GPUaaS)** — renting GPU compute
- **ML infrastructure**
- **Virtual servers + cloud storage**

Operating model: combine co-location at partner datacenters + own specialized facilities.

Recent SEC filings show multiple 8-K disclosures in 2026 — IPO + 8-Ks + private placement + convertible notes — heavy capital-raising activity reflecting early-stage growth ramp.
