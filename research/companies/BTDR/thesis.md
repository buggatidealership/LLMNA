## TL;DR

Bitdeer is a vertically-integrated crypto miner that has **deployed proprietary SEALMINER hardware globally + made a concrete commitment to NVIDIA Vera Rubin co-location**. Q1 2026: revenue $188.9M (+170% YoY, from $70.1M Q1 2025) per [Grafa](https://grafa.com/en/news/united-states/bitdeer-q1-2026-earnings-ai-mining-expansion).

**AI Cloud business ARR ~$69M by April 2026 (+60% MoM)** per same. **Tydal Norway 180 MW datacenter expected complete December 2026, primarily for NVIDIA's Vera Rubin co-location services** per same source.

Q1 self-mining revenue $146.9M (78% of total); AI Cloud $3.7M (Q1) growing to $69M ARR run-rate by April. Net loss $159.5M but Adjusted EBITDA positive $14.4M.

**Aschenbrenner Q1 2026 13F: included (size undisclosed)** per `research/signals/events/2026-05-21-aschenbrenner-q1-13f.md`.

**Position recommendation:** 2-3% if entered — **highest-conviction among the Aschenbrenner smaller names**. Has actual Rubin commitment (rare and material), AI Cloud growing 60% MoM, vertically integrated (own ASIC). Higher upside than CORZ/IREN/APLD because earlier-stage; higher risk because pivot newer.
