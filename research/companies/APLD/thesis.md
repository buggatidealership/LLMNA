## TL;DR

Applied Digital is the HPC datacenter operator with the highest-quality contract book among ex-crypto-miner pivots. **$11B CoreWeave contract at Polaris Forge 1 over ~15 years** + **$5B at Polaris Forge 2** + **$7.5B Delta Forge 1 (300 MW critical IT load) over ~15 years** per [APLD IR](https://ir.applieddigital.com/news-events/press-releases/detail/149/applied-digital-announces-new-u-s-based-high). **Total contracted lease value ~$23.5B+ over multi-year terms.** Aschenbrenner $320M position per `research/signals/events/2026-05-21-aschenbrenner-q1-13f.md`.

## Key facts

Per [APLD Q1 FY26 SEC 8-K via APLD IR](https://ir.applieddigital.com/news-events/press-releases/detail/131/applied-digital-reports-fiscal-first-quarter-2026-results):
- Q1 FY26 revenue: $64.2M (+84% YoY)
- Tenant fit-out services revenue: $26.3M (HPC hosting business)
- Polaris Forge 1: ~$11B contracted revenue over ~15 years (CoreWeave)
- Polaris Forge 2: ~$5B over 15 years (anchor hyperscaler)
- Delta Forge 1: ~$7.5B over 15 years (April 2026, 300 MW IT load, US-based high investment-grade hyperscaler)

## Why this works

Per `research/wiki/power-for-ai-primer.md`: APLD has converted from BTC mining to AI/HPC hosting. Their $23.5B+ contracted revenue book is the largest in the crypto-miner-pivot group. The "Delta Forge 1" Q1 2026 announcement of a NEW hyperscaler customer (not CoreWeave) diversifies customer base materially.

## Anti-fragility

WIN in S3 (power binds). Modest in S1/S2. Anti-fragility 2.5/5.

## Position recommendation

2-4% entry candidate. **Preferred among crypto-miner pivots** because:
- Largest total contracted revenue book ($23.5B+ vs CORZ $10B, IREN $9.7B)
- Multi-customer diversification (CoreWeave + new investment-grade hyperscaler)
- 15-year contract terms = visibility through 2040+
- Already producing material AI/HPC revenue

## Blind spots

- Capex required to fulfill 300 MW Delta Forge 1 + Polaris ramp
- Customer concentration if CoreWeave terminates
- BTC mining residual operations
