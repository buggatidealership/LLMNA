## The business

Powell designs and manufactures custom-engineered medium-voltage switchgear and power control buildings. Products: switchgear assemblies, motor control centers, packaged power solutions. Customers historically dominated by oil & gas (LNG plants, refineries) — datacenter is the new growth vector.

## Why this matters for AI

Per [Simply Wall St narrative](https://simplywall.st/community/narratives/us/capital-goods/nasdaq-powl/powell-industries/4eyswlbc-powell-industries-powl-the-grid-architect-and-the-data-center-megaproject-era): datacenter "megaprojects" (>$75M individual projects) are a new tier of demand that POWL hasn't historically served at scale. The first megaproject win in Q1 FY2026 is a meaningful inflection signal.

Powell's medium-voltage switchgear is increasingly needed as datacenter power densities rise — higher kV products required for GW-scale campuses. This is a leveraged play on the "datacenter as substation" trend.
