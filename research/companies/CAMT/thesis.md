## TL;DR

Camtek is the advanced packaging inspection equipment leader. Per `research/watchlist/candidates.md` (added 2026-05-20): Q1 2026 OSAT orders >$90M for "CoWoS-like packaging for AI" per [StockTitan](https://www.stocktitan.net/sec-filings/CAMT/6-k-camtek-ltd-current-report-foreign-issuer-e53ec04f8240.html). Q2 2026 guide $129-131M revenue per [TipRanks](https://www.tipranks.com/news/company-announcements/camtek-posts-q1-2026-results-and-projects-strong-second-half-revenue-surge). H2 2026 expected to grow >25% vs H1 per same.

## Why this works

Per `research/wiki/hbm-primer.md` + `research/wiki/custom-silicon-primer.md`: every HBM stack + every custom Si chip needs CoWoS-like packaging. CoWoS-L wafers grow from ~55K wpm exit-2025 to ~130-150K wpm exit-2026 per HBM primer. **Every wafer processed needs inspection equipment.** Camtek is a direct, structural beneficiary of TSMC's packaging capacity expansion.

## Anti-fragility

WIN in S1 (NVDA chips need packaging), S2 (custom Si needs packaging). Mixed S3 (not directly tied). Anti-fragility 3/5.

## Position recommendation

2-3% entry candidate. Cleaner pure-play than ex-crypto-miners but smaller TAM. Pair-trade well with AVGO (custom Si design) and SK Hynix (HBM).

## Blind spots
- Stock already moved on the $31M order announcement (Stage 3 entry)
- Competition from Onto Innovation, Camtek's main competitor
- Israeli operations + geopolitical exposure
- Total addressable inspection-equipment market sizing
- Margin profile vs revenue growth
