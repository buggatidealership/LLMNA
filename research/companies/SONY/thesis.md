### The headline finding: Sony FAILS the robotics-vision-bottleneck test — same failure mode as HD

The 2026-05-25 stub framed Sony as "**the strongest single-name answer to universal vision supplier across all Physical AI sub-domains.**" Verification does **not** support the *bottleneck* version of that claim:

1. **Bypass-route FAILS (T1/T2).** Sony is the quality/value leader, **not a chokepoint**. Every imaging modality has ≥2 qualified substitutes:
   - **Premium smartphone:** OmniVision **OV50X** (1-inch, LOFIC HDR) won the **Xiaomi 17 Ultra main camera** — first Chinese sensor in a true halo flagship; Honor Magic 8 Ultra main = OV50R. The "Chinese = low-end only" assumption is **dead** ([中关村在线](https://mobile.zol.com.cn/1089/10899566.html)).
   - **Apple (the crown jewel):** Samsung broke an **18-year Sony iPhone-sensor monopoly** — 48MP ultrawide for iPhone 18, built at **Austin TX**, mass production ~**March 2026** ([EE Times JP](https://eetimes.itmedia.co.jp/ee/articles/2601/15/news062.html), [MacRumors](https://www.macrumors.com/2025/12/24/iphone-18-camera-sensor-to-be-made-in-texas/)). Trigger was Sony's **Nagasaki yield stumble** + inability to match US-made supply politically.
   - **Automotive:** Sony is only **#3** (onsemi ~40%, OmniVision ~28%).
   - **Humanoid:** Optimus camera modules sourced via **Samsung E-M** (Sony sensor not locked); Unitree G1 uses **OmniVision** (RealSense). **No T1 humanoid Sony design-win exists.**

2. **The robotics narrative is a 2030s story, not a current revenue line (T1/T2).** Nikkei xTECH frames SSS humanoid sensors as a 2030s business with zero named humanoid design wins. Bull-case 2028 humanoid CIS revenue ≈ **$60–200M ≈ ~1% of I&SS**. Robotics is **optionality, pre-material.**

3. **"Sandisk/Micron of robotics-vision" archetype = WEAK fit.** Sony matches the **cost side** (sunk capex peaked FY21-23, depreciation rolling off, record 16.6%→~19% margins, explicit capex discipline) but **NOT the revenue side**: no LTAs/take-or-pay/prepayments (design-win renewed per iPhone generation), customer concentration now moving *against* it (Apple dual-sourcing), and **mix-led ASP gains — NOT shortage-pricing**. The memory crunch that *helps* the Micron archetype is a **demand headwind** for Sony mobile CIS (raises smartphone BOM, moderates sensor-size growth).

**This is the third robotics-supplier bypass-route correction in the cohort** (Nidec, HD, now Sony) — see META-PATTERN "robotics-localization-blind-spot" in `meta/structural-winners-cohort.md`.

### The thesis that SURVIVES: premium-imaging compounder + auto kicker + SOTP crystallization

1. **Premium-imaging process moat is real and durable (T1/T2).** Only **Sony + Samsung** can commercialize **3-wafer (PD/transistor/logic) stacking**. Collapse risk low. Realistic path = gradual value-share compression from ~53% toward mid-40s as Apple dual-sources — **"leader under siege," not "leader displaced."** Retire the old "43–64%" range; **anchor on Sony's own T1: 53% FY24 value share, 56% FY25 guide** (the 60% target was officially pushed out "several years," citing Apple weakness + Chinese high-end competition).

2. **Automotive is the one REAL AI vector (T1).** Value share **37% (FY24) → 39% (FY25) → 43% target (FY26)**; ~90% of major OEMs to adopt Sony auto CIS by FY26; cameras/car **3.5 → 6.5 by FY2030** (+~80% content). Auto turns profitable FY26. This is structural, not narrative.

3. **SOTP crystallization actively underway (T1/T2):**
   - Financial Services **partial spin-off completed Oct 1, 2025** (Sony retains 16.4%, equity-method).
   - TV → **Bravia Inc. JV** (TCL 51%) signed Mar 2026.
   - **AFEELA EV canceled Mar 25, 2026** — loss removal.
   - **Sony–TSMC image-sensor JV MOU (May 8, 2026, Sony majority/controlling)** at new Koshi/Kumamoto fab, explicitly targeting "physical AI / automotive / robotics" — a **fab-lite partial-I&SS-crystallization path** (full SSS spinoff *considered, not adopted*; CEO Totoki denied broader spinoffs).
   - Nomura/Wolfe ¥5,300 (+51%) on "richer SOTP"; consensus ¥4,676 (+36%) → implied ~25–35% conglomerate discount **(T3 — context only, not anchor).**

## POSITION IMPLICATION

**No action / do NOT add on the robotics-vision thesis.** That thesis failed the same bypass-route test that took down Nidec and HD. **Sony does not belong in the structural-winner cohort as a robotics-bottleneck name.**

Sony *is* worth tracking as a **separate, cheaper value-catalyst play**: premium-imaging leader (process moat real, eroding at edges) + structural automotive content growth + active SOTP crystallization at a ~25–35% conglomerate discount, fwd ~17x, with a buyback and a clean ¥3,390 technical line. That is a **value/catalyst archetype, not an AI-bottleneck archetype** — and it should only be sized if/when the SOTP/auto re-rating becomes the explicit thesis, not as a backdoor robotics bet.

**Falsifiers for the SURVIVING thesis:**
1. Value share breaks below ~45% (vs Sony's own 53%/56%) over 2+ prints → "leader under siege" tips to "leader displaced."
2. Samsung wins an iPhone **main** (not ultrawide) socket by 2027 → Apple-concentration crack widens materially.
3. Price loses ¥3,390 on a closing basis → L24 post-break thesis void.
4. SOTP crystallization stalls (TSMC JV non-binding MOU lapses; no further segment action 12mo) → discount-closure catalyst gone.
5. Automotive value share fails to reach ~43% FY26 → the one real AI vector stalls.

**Catalysts to watch:** TSMC JV definitive deal (months); Q1 FY26 earnings ~Aug 2026; Samsung Austin iPhone-18 line ramp (March 2026 — falsifier #2 tracker); PS6 reveal (2027–28, memory-cost risk); auto-segment FY26 profitability print.
