## The business

Bloom Energy (NYSE: BE) makes solid oxide fuel cells (SOFCs). The "Energy Server" product:
- Modular, fuel cell-based power generation
- Behind-the-meter deployment (no grid interconnect required)
- Months-not-years deployment time
- Runs on natural gas, biogas, or hydrogen
- Higher efficiency + lower emissions than gas turbines per unit MW

Why this matters for AI: per `research/wiki/power-for-ai-primer.md`, grid interconnect queues are 36-48 months in PJM/ERCOT data center zones; gas turbine backlogs sold out through 2030. Bloom can deploy in months. Hyperscalers pay a premium for time-to-power.
