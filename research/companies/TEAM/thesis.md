## TL;DR

Atlassian is the Jira + Confluence + Bitbucket collaboration platform with **rapidly growing agentic AI products (Rovo)**. Q3 FY2026: revenue $1,787M (+32% YoY), cloud revenue +29% YoY, Service Collection >$1B ARR (+30% YoY) per [Q3 8-K via SEC](https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/0001650372/000165037226000024/ex991q3fy26.htm) + [BusinessWire](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260430534706/en/Atlassian-Announces-Third-Quarter-Fiscal-Year-2026-Results).

**Key AI signal:** Rovo (AI product) surpassed **5 million monthly active users** after first $1B Cloud-revenue quarter in Q2 FY26. Rovo Dev agent in Jira lets developers delegate routine tasks (security fixes, migrations, feature-flag cleanups) per [Timothy Sykes](https://www.timothysykes.com/news/atlassian-corporation-team-news-2026_05_15/). FY2026 guide: mid-20s percentage revenue growth.

**Position recommendation:** 2-4% if entered. **Strongest agentic-AI product traction** outside the model providers among public companies. Pairs with held DDOG (observability) and held NOW (workflow) — agentic-AI software thesis stack.

## The business

Per [Q3 FY26 BusinessWire](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260430534706/en/Atlassian-Announces-Third-Quarter-Fiscal-Year-2026-Results):
- **Teamwork Collection** — Jira, Confluence, Loom (productivity + collaboration)
- **Service Collection** — Jira Service Management, Customer Service Management, Assets (ITSM/CSM)
- **Rovo** — AI agent layer cutting across all products

Customer base: ~300K+ enterprises; strong developer footprint.

## Why this matters for AI

Per `research/wiki/agentic-ai-enterprise.md`: agentic AI is the 12% breakthrough pattern. Atlassian's Rovo is one of the few enterprise products with **measurable agentic adoption (5M MAU)** and clear use cases (Rovo Dev for software engineering, Remix for content transformation).

The 32% topline growth + 29% cloud growth (per Q3 8-K) accelerated specifically as Rovo adoption ramped. AI is the marginal growth lever — quantified as "increased AI capabilities" driving Jira seat expansion per BusinessWire.
