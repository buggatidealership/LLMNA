- 35% RV reducer market share industrial robots + 28% miniature RV humanoid market share verified
- **Capacity-doubling announcement by 2026** (Nabtesco IR 2025 Dec) = execution discipline + demand confidence signal
- Humanoid reducer market 46.3% CAGR ($52M → $580M by 2032)

## Bear case
- Capacity doubling may compress pricing — HDS's pricing-power thesis (capacity-constrained, lead-times 6mo+) doesn't apply post-ramp
- Chinese localization takes domestic Chinese market
- Tesla 90%+ miss on 2025 = demand from largest hyped Western OEM weaker than expected
- Same Schaeffler entrant risk as HDS in humanoid actuators
- Section 232 / China tariff exposure (Japanese supplier with Chinese customer flow)

## Base case
- Capacity ramp completes ahead of full humanoid demand materialization → muted earnings growth 2026-2027
- Pricing premium intact on Western OEMs that haven't qualified Chinese alternatives
- Multi-product launches (RVmini + Monocrank) maintain non-humanoid revenue stability

## Falsifiers
1. **Capacity-doubling timeline slips OR is reversed** (capex announcement walked back) → execution confidence breaks
2. **Tesla / Figure / Apptronik commits to Chinese reducer supply** (qualification crossing) → Western humanoid bypass-route validated
3. **Pricing compression visible in earnings calls** (margin guidance down) post-ramp → capacity-doubling thesis-negative
4. **Humanoid commercial deployment >100K units/year within 18 months** (positive falsifier — accelerates demand)
