## The business

T1 Energy (NYSE: TE), formerly FREYR Battery, pivoted business model in late 2024 from battery manufacturing to US-domestic solar manufacturing. Per [Bitget overview](https://www.bitget.com/wiki/t1-energy-stock):
- US-based solar cell + module manufacturer
- Primary asset: G1_Dallas (Texas) photovoltaic module facility, 5 GW nameplate capacity
- Under construction: G2_Austin solar cell fab, 2.1 GW first phase, target late 2026 production

Per `research/wiki/power-for-ai-primer.md`:

1. Hyperscaler clean-power demand
2. US tariff barrier vs Chinese solar imports
3. IRA Section 45X production tax credits
