## The business

Tower Semiconductor (NASDAQ: TSEM, also TASE) is an Israeli specialty foundry. NOT a leading-edge logic foundry (TSMC's space). Instead: specialty processes for analog, RF, power, MEMS, sensors, and — critically — **silicon photonics**.

Per `wiki/optical-interconnect-primer.md`: silicon photonics is the foundation of the CPO (co-packaged optics) transition. Specialty foundries like Tower are where silicon photonics chips are manufactured.

Tower's silicon photonics platform (PH18) is one of the most-deployed silicon photonics processes globally. Customers include hyperscalers + optical companies + AI infrastructure providers.
