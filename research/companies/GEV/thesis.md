## The business

## Why this matters for AI

Per `research/wiki/power-for-ai-primer.md`: AI datacenters need MW-to-GW scale gas turbines for behind-the-meter generation when grid interconnect is multi-year delayed (the time-to-power thesis). Gas turbine OEMs are capacity-constrained — orders today ship 2028-2030. GEV is one of only 3 Western players who can ship at scale.

The $2.4B Q1 2026 datacenter equipment orders in Electrification segment (per Yahoo Finance) is the direct AI signal. The 17 GW step-up in gas turbine backlog in one quarter (83 → 100 GW per Motley Fool) is the indirect AI signal.
