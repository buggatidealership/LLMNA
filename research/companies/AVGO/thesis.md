## Duration × Magnitude × Pricing × Recognition × Execution Quality

### Duration of constraint (custom Si tailwind)
Hyperscaler custom Si programs are multi-year commitments. Per Custom Silicon Primer: 44.6% CAGR through end of decade. **Duration: 5+ years** of structural tailwind.

### Magnitude
CEO guidance: >$100B AI chip revenue by 2027 vs ~$30-40B run-rate FY26. That's roughly 2.5-3× growth over 2-year window. Operating leverage: software margin model (60%+ gross) for design fees + recurring royalties.

### Pricing power
AVGO's pricing power on custom Si is HIGH for THREE reasons:
1. **Few credible alternatives** — only MRVL is a credible second source for the design partnership model
2. **Hyperscaler switching cost** — each ASIC takes 2-3 years to design + qualify; switching design houses mid-cycle is impractical
3. **Bundled IP advantage** — AVGO owns critical networking IP that complements custom AI silicon

### Recognition Stage
- 12 months ago: Stage 3
- 6 months ago: Stage 3-4
- Today: **Stage 4** (multi-year sell-side coverage; ~$1.2T+ market cap per user reference; multiple expanded materially)
- Stage 5 risk: real but mitigated by accelerating AI revenue growth

**Stage 4 means:** multiple compression risk is real, but absolute earnings growth is fast enough to defend the multiple in 2026-2027. This is a "grow into the multiple" story.

## Bull case (P = 50%)

AVGO AI revenue scales to $50-60B+ in FY27, matching or exceeding CEO's $100B-by-2027 line-of-sight. Custom Si continues taking share from NVDA on inference. Multiple sustains or expands as 2027 forward visibility solidifies. **Expected return: +30% to +70% over 18 months.**

## Bear case (P = 20%)

S4 (digestion) plays — hyperscaler capex pauses, custom Si programs slow. Multiple compresses materially given Stage 4 entry. **Expected loss: -25% to -40%.**

## Base case (P = 30%)

AI revenue grows ~70% in FY27 (deceleration from current +106% pace). Multiple flat-to-modestly-down. Stock +15% to +30%.

## Falsifiers — mandatory

1. **Q3 FY26 AI revenue growth slows below 50% YoY.** Would suggest the custom Si ramp is moderating; bear case probability rises.
2. **A major hyperscaler announces design house switch (AVGO → MRVL or in-house).** Customer relationships less sticky than modeled.
3. **NVDA Rubin / next-gen efficiency advantage proves so large custom Si TCO arbitrage disappears.** Structural moat breaks.
4. **TSMC packaging allocation favors NVDA over AVGO custom Si programs.** Custom Si supply lags demand.
