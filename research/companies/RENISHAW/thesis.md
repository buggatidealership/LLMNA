### The headline finding: Renishaw FAILS humanoid bypass-route — same pattern, same failure mode as Nidec/HDS/Sony

The 2026-06-09 AM watchlist entry framed Renishaw as "**PASSES bypass-route test at high-precision tier (sensorless control limited; Chinese alternatives at low-end only); 8/9 Sandisk/Micron archetype criteria.**" Verification kills both halves of that claim:

1. **The wrong-tech error.** Humanoid joints do NOT use the optical/inductive grating-scale technology where Renishaw + Heidenhain dominate (>65% China grating-scale per T2 [Minsheng Securities](https://pdf.dfcfw.com/pdf/H3_AP202305291587333090_1.pdf); top-3 ~70% in linear scale niche per T3). Every surveyed humanoid actuator (Tesla Optimus, Figure 02/03, Apollo, Unitree G1/H1, 1X, Fourier, XPeng Iron) uses **off-axis MAGNETIC absolute encoders — AMS AS5047P-class single-chip ICs at $5–15** (T1/T2 multiple teardowns + open-source actuator references). Encoders are **only ~4.45% of Optimus third-party BOM** (T2 [optimusk.blog](https://optimusk.blog/blog/tesla-optimus-hardware-specs/)). The optical-encoder moat is in the wrong product category for humanoids.

2. **The Chinese-localization error.** Mandatory native-Chinese searches kill the "Chinese only at low-end" premise FOR HUMANOIDS — Unitree G1 uses **self-developed dual encoders** in QDD joint modules (T2 [Unitree G1 teardown CSDN](https://blog.csdn.net/u010443710/article/details/153753058)); **Inovance 汇川 delivered first-gen self-developed encoders + joint modules in 2025** (T1 [Inovance 2025 Annual Report](https://static.cninfo.com.cn/finalpage/2026-04-28/1225208487.PDF)); Aopu/Yuheng 奥普光电/禹衡光学 explicitly told investors March 2025 their high-end encoders are "暂未直接给人形机器人厂家供货" (not yet directly supplied to humanoid OEMs — T1 [财联社](https://www.cls.cn/detail/1395749)) yet they are pre-qualified at the joint-module tier via Inovance.

3. **Verified Renishaw humanoid design-wins:** Only **PAL Robotics REEM-C / TALOS** (knee/wrist/elbow via RLS AksIM/Orbis — T1 [Renishaw case study](https://www.renishaw.com/en/pal-robotics-integrates-magnetic-encoder-technology-into-robots-to-achieve-balance--43036)) + TQ-RoboDrive actuator + UR cobots. **NO Tesla / Figure / Apptronik / Unitree / XPeng / Fourier / 1X / BD design-win exists.** PAL Robotics is not a T1 humanoid platform.

4. **The non-trivial nuance.** Renishaw's majority-owned subsidiary **RLS** IS in the magnetic-encoder substitute stack (AksIM, Orbis, AksIM-4 Dual Concentric launched Automate 2026). So Renishaw participates — but as one commodity-adjacent vendor among AMS-Osram, Allegro, MPS, Melexis, Inovance — **not a chokepoint.** Build (my model): 2026 humanoid encoder revenue ~$5m (20k units × 32 encoder-points × 10% attach × $80); 2030 stretch ~£110m only if RLS holds attach against Chinese price compression (P≤35%, my model).

5. **Factual error in my own watchlist source** (now flagged for correction): I had written "Heidenhain parent = ITK Engineering AG" — wrong. T1 [Heidenhain corporate profile](https://www.heidenhain.com/about-us/corporate-profile): Heidenhain is owned by **Dr. Johannes Heidenhain-Stiftung**. ITK Engineering AG is a separate Bosch subsidiary.
