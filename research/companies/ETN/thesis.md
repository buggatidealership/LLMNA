## The business

Eaton operates across two main segments per Q1 2026 8-K cited in [BigGo](https://finance.biggo.com/news/US_ETN_2026-05-05):
- **Electrical** — power management, distribution, protection (~70% revenue; most AI exposure)
- **Aerospace, Vehicle, eMobility** — non-AI segments

Within Electrical: medium-voltage switchgear, UPS systems, busways, power distribution units (PDUs) — all critical to datacenter buildouts.

## Why this matters for AI

Per `research/wiki/power-for-ai-primer.md` and Eaton's own framing per [Globe and Mail](https://www.theglobeandmail.com/investing/markets/stocks/ETN/pressreleases/1758738/eaton-earnings-call-highlights-data-center-supercycle/): "data center supercycle." Unlike GEV (gas generation), ETN sits at the **grid-to-chip** layer — the electrical infrastructure inside the datacenter. Every hyperscaler buildout requires their products.
