## Why the agentic AI thesis is intact

Per `research/wiki/agentic-ai-enterprise.md`:
- Successful agentic deployments share 5 traits: narrow scope, tight feedback loop, **workflow integration**, eval infrastructure, human-in-the-loop initially
- "Vertical SaaS gets a second life" is one of the non-default reads (per `sector/where-we-are.md` non-default reads)

ServiceNow checks every box:
- Owns the enterprise workflow layer (customers have years of process built in NOW)
- Agentforce integrates agents directly into existing workflows
- Switching costs are extreme (rebuilding workflows elsewhere takes years)
- Existing customer relationships at >80% of Fortune 500

This makes NOW one of the most defensible AI-software plays. Even if frontier models commoditize at the bottom of the stack, NOW's workflow + customer relationship moat persists.
