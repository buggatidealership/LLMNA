## TL;DR
**The cleanest international AI-execution proof point in the entire 8-layer cohort.** EndoBRAIN-EYE (PMDA-cleared 2020, first-in-class CADe colonoscopy) earned **保険適用 reimbursement 2024-06: +60点 (¥600) add-on K721 polypectomy** — Japan's national payer literally wrote a NEW reimbursement code SPECIFICALLY for diagnostic AI add-ons. This + Heartflow CPT 75580 are the canonical "reimbursement-coded AI" cases that validate the framework's load-bearing prior. Olympus owns ~70% global endoscopy share — the AI rides a deep hardware install-base moat.

## Why Olympus is structurally important to the medical-AI thesis
- Validates the mechanism that **national payers will write new codes for diagnostic AI add-ons** — Japan moved first, Heartflow's CPT 75580 (US) shows the same path. International reimbursement expansion is the structural enabler for the entire L5 diagnostics layer
- AI segment is currently a SMALL slice of the ¥-trillions endoscopy franchise — but the trajectory is what matters per principle #22 (segment trajectory not snapshot)
- ⚠️ Segment-trajectory framing required: AI is a small percentage of group revenue today; the investable thesis is forward-modeling AI add-on penetration across the install base over 24-36 months, NOT current snapshot AI-revenue percentage

## Bull case (P~40%, my model)
- EndoBRAIN reimbursement code path replicates in US/EU/CN (already 70% global share; the reimbursement infrastructure follows the hardware footprint)
- Cross-modality CADe expansion (gastric, esophageal, upper-GI) leveraging the same installed scope base
- Endoscopy procedure volume secular growth (aging populations, colorectal cancer screening guideline expansion)

## Bear case (P~25%, my model)
- CADe competition from medtronic GI Genius + Pentax DISCOVERY commoditizes the AI feature
- Japanese reimbursement cycle compresses ¥600 add-on within 2-3 review cycles
- Foundation-model CADe (general-purpose colonoscopy AI from cloud providers) commoditizes the algorithm

## Base case (P~35%, my model)
- Steady endoscopy franchise compounding; AI segment grows but stays a single-digit percentage of group revenue; thesis works only on the existing endoscopy investment merits, not the AI overlay

## Falsifiers
1. Japan 厚労省 cuts the +60点 add-on at next reimbursement review
2. Medtronic GI Genius or Pentax DISCOVERY achieves comparable CADe reimbursement in Japan
3. Olympus loses endoscopy share materially below 65%
4. International reimbursement-code expansion fails to replicate beyond Japan within 24 months
