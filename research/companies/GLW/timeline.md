## Pre-2025 — Brief context

- **2022-2024:** Corning shifted strategy from pandemic-era TV/display headwinds toward AI infrastructure. Optical Communications growth accelerated as hyperscaler datacenter buildout began.
- **2010s era:** Gorilla Glass dominance built Corning's specialty-materials moat. Optical fiber business steady but not the growth driver it has become.
- **Historic moat:** SMF-28 optical fiber (industry-standard low-loss single-mode fiber) has been Corning's flagship for decades.
