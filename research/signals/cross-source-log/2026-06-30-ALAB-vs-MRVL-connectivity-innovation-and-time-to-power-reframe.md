## PART 1 — ALAB vs MRVL connectivity innovation (Subagent A, research-verified 2026-06-30 T1/T2)

**Both are boundary-pushers — but the boundary SPLIT into two lanes (electrical vs optical scale-up); they each lead a different one.**

| Axis | ALAB (Astera Labs) | MRVL (Marvell) |
|---|---|---|
| Stack | Connectivity-PURE: Aries retimers, Taurus Ethernet, Leo CXL, **Scorpio P/X fabric switches**, optical PCIe, COSMOS sw | Connectivity + custom-ASIC: **optical DSP ~70% share**, Inphi electro-optics, **Celestial AI photonic fabric**, AEC, coherent DCI |
| Frontier bet | **Electrical scale-up** (Scorpio 320-lane, NVLink Fusion, UALink, PCIe) | **Optical scale-out + optical scale-up** (1.6T DSP, Celestial copper→optics) |
| Innovation class (home turf) | **BOUNDARY-PUSHER** electrical scale-up switching (~75%) | **BOUNDARY-PUSHER** optical DSP/photonic fabric (~70%) |
| Standard-server half | none (pure) | **core DSP serves 800G/1.6T pluggable duopoly w/ AVGO** = the CPO/LPO-threatened half |
| Purity | **PUREST connectivity-innovation expression (~80%)** | diluted by custom-ASIC (lost Trainium 3/4 to Alchip) |
| Mkt cap (≈ Jun-2026, directional) | ~$77B, rev +93% YoY, 76% GM | ~$230B, rev +63% YoY |
| Key threat | **~70% single-customer (AWS) concentration**; NVIDIA in-sourcing retimers/switches; AVGO entering PCIe retimers | CPO/LPO disintermediating pluggable DSP; AVGO DSP competition; Celestial integration risk |

**Innovation verdict:** ALAB is the cleaner BOUNDARY-PUSHER — **Scorpio is a genuinely NEW merchant category** (320-lane AI scale-up switch that didn't exist as a buyable part = defining a surface, not serving a spec) and it's **protocol-agnostic** (wins under NVLink Fusion, UALink, AND PCIe) = scenario-robust. MRVL pushes the optical boundary (1.6T DSP first-mover + Celestial photonic fabric) but its DSP cash cow is the standard-server half CPO commoditizes, and custom-ASIC losses muddy the read.

**Scale-up battleground (bypass-route framing, Critical Rule #9):** the consensus scale-up solution = NVIDIA-proprietary NVLink. The BYPASS = merchant protocol-agnostic fabric letting hyperscalers build non-NVLink scale-up domains.
- **Short-reach / in-rack (copper, electrical): ALAB favored (~70%)** = bottleneck-of-today, bought NOW.
- **Long-reach / multi-rack (optical): MRVL/Celestial favored (~55%)** = bottleneck-of-2027-28, larger but later + execution-risked (Celestial pre-revenue).

**Cascade read:** ALAB = the purest scenario-robust connectivity-innovation bet (watchlist; >$50B cap + prior SKIP-SIZE flag). **MRVL re-entry lens SHARPENED: the connectivity bull case for Marvell is specifically the Celestial/OPTICAL-scale-up leg, NOT the DSP duopoly (the commoditizing half)** — refines the 2026-06-30 connectivity re-entry note.
