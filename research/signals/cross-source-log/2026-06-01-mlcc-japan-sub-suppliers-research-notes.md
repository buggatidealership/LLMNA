### MLCC supply chain position (verified)

- **Electronic paste / thick film for MLCC internal electrodes**: Noritake product catalog explicitly lists "Paste for Electronic Components" + "Electronic Paste (Thick Film)" for MLCC applications. Holds patents on nickel paste with <100ppm sulfur for MLCC internal electrodes.
- **Thick film circuit substrates**: ceramic substrates used as packaging/circuit boards in automotive, medical, LED systems — indirectly MLCC-ecosystem adjacent
- **NOT a primary dielectric powder supplier**: barium titanate (BaTiO3) primary MLCC dielectric is captive to MLCC makers OR sourced from Sakai Chemical / Fuji Titanium. Noritake sits at paste/substrate layer, ONE STEP DOWNSTREAM from primary raw materials.
- **MLCC customers**: NOT publicly disclosed in English sources. Murata/TDK/Samsung Electro-Mechanics/Yageo not named explicitly as customers
- **Competitive position at electronic paste**: vs DuPont, Heraeus, Sumitomo Metal Mining — niche specialist, not clear global #1
- **Pricing power evidence**: NONE directly verified; inferred from MLCC qualification cycle switching costs (multi-year design-in)
