## LEG 2 — RETRODICTION 2007 (returned; ~49.5k, 14 tool uses)

### The surviving method: **PCPL — Primitive → Cost-Collapse → Precedent → Lag-clock**
1. GENERATE from the platform's spec sheet + economics (never launch-day apps): the new irreducible primitives at scale. 2008 primitives, all provably public then: camera-in-pocket, persistent GPS (Jobs on record Jun-08), contact graph, push (WWDC-08), payments-on-file (iTunes cards), one-tap distribution, + the visible behavioral tailwind (SMS +160% YoY at 20¢ carrier-gouging = the WhatsApp arbitrage sitting in plain sight).
2. DOUBLE GATE each primitive→application mapping: (a) COST-COLLAPSE — a real cost drops ~10x toward zero (photo→IG, message-vs-20¢-SMS→WhatsApp, dispatch→Uber); (b) BEHAVIORAL PRECEDENT — the behavior already exists, friction-gated; net-new behavior with no analog = REJECT.
3. REJECT rules (the loser-gate, honestly derivable ex-ante): primitive not yet at scale (Glass, QR-West); no-analog behavior (Color: photographing co-located strangers; check-in as an end); cold-start with no bootstrap graph (Color's 150-ft density problem); fights the platform owner (mobile Flash, mobile-web-maximalism betting against sensor/push/payment access).
4. LAG CLOCK — **two wave regimes:** Wave 1: primitive ships → founding ~1yr later (WhatsApp waited for push 2009) → scale 2-5yr (software fast, physical-world slow: IG 2yr, Uber 5yr). **Wave 2: the biggest winner (TikTok) needed primitives that DIDN'T EXIST at platform launch** (front-cam ubiquity + LTE video + feed-ranking ML, matured 2014-16) → platform-to-winner ~9-11yr. A 2008 forecaster could NOT have named TikTok — the method's correct move is to FLAG the immature primitive and defer, not guess.
5. Expert-failure direction confirmed: 2007-09 discourse was platform-SHARE wars (Nokia modeled Apple at 0.6-1.1%) + "fart-app" dismissal; nobody reasoned from address-book+camera+push to two billion-user messengers.

### Contamination statement (agent's, honest): primitive→winner mapping is hindsight-drawn even though inputs are provably 2008-public; rejection rules partly clean (cold-start + no-precedent ARE ex-ante derivable); lag numbers are survivorship-biased (describe when winners scaled, not P(success|entry)).
