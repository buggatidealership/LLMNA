## 5. User hypothesis verification: "open-source monetizes worse than closed-source"

🔴 **HYPOTHESIS PARTIALLY FALSIFIED. The license dimension is not the structural variable that explains monetization differences.**

| Evidence | Direction |
|---|---|
| Both Zhipu (MIT) and MiniMax (open-weights w/ commercial-gating) growing rev 130-160% YoY | Hypothesis would predict Zhipu lags MiniMax materially — actual data shows they're comparable |
| Zhipu absolute revenue ~25% larger than MiniMax in 2025 (RMB 720M vs ~570M equiv) | DIRECTLY contradicts hypothesis at the absolute-revenue level |
| MiniMax has stronger UNIT-revenue advantage (cheaper API pricing $0.30 vs $1.00 input) | License is irrelevant — MiniMax's edge is pricing aggressiveness + multimodal product mix, NOT closed-source distribution |
| Zhipu's 41% gross margin is enviable for an open-weights player | Strongly contradicts hypothesis — open-source allows commodity-substrate competition that COMPRESSES gross margin in theory; Zhipu's 41% shows the B2B-custom-deployment model OFFSETS this entirely |
| MiniMax has LARGER net loss expansion (302%) than Zhipu (60%) | License is not predictive of loss profile; capital deployment intensity is |
| Anthropic + OpenAI (closed-source) DO monetize better in absolute terms | But at >100× the revenue base; at the $50-100M revenue scale, license model is dominated by GTM strategy, product mix, geographic reach |

**REAL EXPLANATORY VARIABLES (replacing the user's license hypothesis):**

1. **Customer type** — B2B-sovereign (Zhipu) vs B2C-consumer (MiniMax) drives different cost structure and revenue ramp shape
2. **Geographic mix** — 73% international (MiniMax) vs mostly-China (Zhipu) drives FX-denominated ARPU + market access risk
3. **Product breadth** — multimodal (Hailuo video) gives MiniMax higher ARPU surface vs text-only Zhipu
4. **Pricing strategy** — MiniMax's 3-4× cheaper API is a deliberate market-share grab; license is downstream of this

**Refined hypothesis user should test going forward:** "B2C consumer-funnel models monetize faster but at lower gross margin than B2B sovereign-deployment models" — the data supports this much better than the open-source/closed-source axis.
