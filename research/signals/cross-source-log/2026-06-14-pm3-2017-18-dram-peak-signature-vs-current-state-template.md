## 2. Universals vs cycle-specific framework (user framework correction integrated)

### UNIVERSALS — physics-of-cycles signals that will fire AT ANY cycle peak in form

| Universal | Mechanism | Why universal |

| **U1 Spot price < contract price** | Marginal-buyer demand softens; spot reflects real-time demand faster than negotiated contracts | Basic commodity market microstructure; firing pattern recurs across DRAM/NAND/memory cycles 1995/2000/2007/2018 |
| **U2 First segment failure** | Marginal demand segment fails first; cycle weakness shows in least-sticky tier before broad-based | Demand isn't homogeneous; weakest segment leads (was graphics/crypto 2018; will be different segment 2024-26) |
| **U3 Revenue composition shifts ASP→volume** | When pricing power exhausts but units still grow, revenue growth becomes volume-only | Universal pricing-power exhaustion signal at any commodity peak |
| **U4 Stock leads ASP by 3-4 months** | Equity markets price 6-mo forward; cycle peak prices in before contract data confirms | Universal market behavior at cyclical peaks across sectors, not just memory |
| **U5 First analyst downgrade is VALUATION-based, not cycle-end** | Analysts lag fundamental data; first downgrade frames as "priced in" not "cycle over" | Universal analyst behavior — Morgan Stanley 2018 + Goldman 2007 + every prior cycle confirmed pattern |
| **U6 Supplier guidance shifts "tight"→"balanced market approaching"** | Suppliers acknowledge weakness AFTER it shows in their order book | Universal disclosure-lag pattern |
