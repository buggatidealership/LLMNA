## 🚩 FRAMING-ERROR CAUGHT (the precursor was partly a misread)
Phase-1 Section 232 (Proclamation 11002, **25%, in effect 2026-01-15**, T1 White House) covers only advanced AI **LOGIC** accelerators (NVIDIA H200, AMD MI325X) defined by **TPP + DRAM-bandwidth thresholds**. The "DRAM bandwidth" criterion **describes the logic accelerator's spec (which chips are covered) — it is NOT a tariff on DRAM/HBM as a product.** Standalone memory dies/modules/SSDs sit under different HTS codes and are NOT the dutiable item. The KJ Leg B "memory in scope" read mis-mapped that threshold. (T1-legal: EY, White & Case.)
