## 4. Glass-Core / CoPoS Displacement Timing — PRIMARY WATCH

### Industry-consensus timeline (TrendForce, DigiTimes, BigGo convergence — T2 high-confidence):

| Year | Milestone |
|---|---|
| 2026 | Validation period (equipment, materials) |
| 2027 | Pilot / small-volume trial production |
| 2028-2029 | Mass production ramp (CoPoS) |
| 2030+ | Glass-core fully commercial at scale |

TSMC Chairman C.C. Wei (June 2026 shareholder meeting, T1): "We already have a pilot line. I guess it will take another two to three years before the volume becomes very large." Pilot line at VisEra, mid-2026 completion.

### IBIDEN's positioning — DECISIVE FINDING

**TSMC formally announced (June 2026) IBIDEN + Innolux as glass-core CoPoS co-development partners.** Source-tier T1 (TSMC announcement covered by DigiTimes, TradingKey, Ming-Chi Kuo). Architecture:

- **3-layer design: ABF / glass-core / ABF**
- IBIDEN supplies the ABF layers (its core expertise)
- Innolux supplies the glass core (panel-maker expertise)
- TSMC assembles

This INVERTS the displacement narrative. IBIDEN is not being displaced by glass — IBIDEN is monetizing glass adoption. Validation results: warpage -16%, CTE -19%, modulus +31%.

### Falsifier-trigger window (revised)

The N-th order bear thesis (glass displaces FC BGA -> IBIDEN demand collapses) requires:
- Pure-glass substrates (no ABF) at scale, AND
- IBIDEN's ABF expertise non-transferable to glass-layer integration

Both conditions look unlikely through 2029. **Falsifier-trigger watch: 2H 2028 — if TSMC commercializes a non-ABF glass variant that excludes IBIDEN, thesis breaks.** Until then, glass-core is additive.

### Competitive read on glass:
- **SEMCO (KR):** Pilot at Sejong; mass production targeted 2027; samples to customers in 2025. T1.
- **Absolics (SKC subsidiary):** Entered AMD certification at Georgia Covington fab. T2.
- **Intel:** January 2026 NEPCON debut of EMIB + glass-core sample. T1.
- **Samsung:** Project shifted to business unit; 2027 ramp target. T1.

Korean glass push is real but the ecosystem winners are different from FC-BGA winners — IBIDEN is in BOTH camps.
