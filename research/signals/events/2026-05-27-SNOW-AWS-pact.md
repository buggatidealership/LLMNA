Per [BusinessWire T1](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260527269473/en/Snowflake-Expands-AWS-Collaboration-with-$6B-Commitment-to-Accelerate-Enterprise-Agentic-AI-Adoption): "Snowflake is making a $6 billion multi-year infrastructure commitment to AWS, its largest to date."

**Layered on top**: multi-year SCA includes co-sell motion, AWS Marketplace expansion, product integrations across gen AI and agentic AI, and joint customer-success investments.

**Implication framing**: This is SNOW's COST commitment, not revenue commitment. It signals confidence in demand durability and locks in compute capacity for the agentic AI demand surge. The MOD $4B Airedale parallel is **WRONG** — MOD's $4B was a customer paying MOD (revenue commitment); SNOW's $6B is SNOW paying AWS (cost commitment). Opposite-direction signals.

The better structural parallel is **OpenAI-AWS** (AI software company committing multi-year spend to hyperscaler for capacity security), not Microsoft-OpenAI (Microsoft investing IN OpenAI).
