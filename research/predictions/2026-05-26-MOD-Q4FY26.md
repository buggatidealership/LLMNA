### 1. Revenue beat or miss?
**Prediction: BEAT.** Point estimate $940M (range $905-985M); consensus $920.7M.

### 2. EPS beat or miss?
**Prediction: SLIGHT BEAT.** Point estimate $1.52-1.58; consensus $1.55.
- **P(beat EPS): ~65-70%** (estimate, lower confidence than revenue because margin trajectory has more uncertainty)
- **Magnitude estimate: in-line to +5% above consensus** = $1.55-$1.63 range likely

- Falsifier: Climate Solutions adj EBITDA margin does NOT recover the guided +200bps sequential improvement; OR capacity-ramp absorption math worse than mgmt indicated

### 3. Guidance raise probability?
**Prediction: HIGH likelihood mgmt raises OR provides initial FY27 guide ABOVE consensus.**
- **P(FY27 guide above pre-deal consensus): ~85-90%** (estimate)
- Rationale: Modine raised FY26 guide TWICE in FY26 (Q2 + Q3); $4B deal disclosed today exceeds prior FY28 DC target ($2B) implicitly accelerated to FY27
- Mgmt also guided 50-70% DC CAGR for next two years (FY27-FY28) per Q3 call

- FY27 DC revenue likely guided >$1.5B (was implied $1.3-1.5B from FY28 $2B target prior to deal); now potentially $1.7-2B+ with the new deal incorporated
- Falsifier: mgmt chooses conservative FY27 framework citing spin-off complexity OR provides only directional FY27 commentary without numeric guide

### 4. Profit margin direction?
