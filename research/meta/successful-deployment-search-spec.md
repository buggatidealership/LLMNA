# SUCCESSFUL-DEPLOYMENT SEARCH SPEC — evidence collection for the end-demand-durability model (codified 2026-07-09 NIGHT, user question)

**Design principle:** announcements are free, pilots are cheap; SUCCESS leaves economic residue that cannot be faked. Search the residue, never the press release. **Success test (all four): past tense + recurring payment + named counterparty + survived a renewal.**

## The eight search classes (residue-targeted)
1. **Earnings-call money trail:** transcripts, "AI" + $ figures in PAST TENSE ("generated/saved/attributed") under securities-law exposure. Discard future-tense ("expect/plan/pilot"). Sources: call transcripts, 10-Q/K MD&A.
2. **Renewal evidence:** multi-year renewals, seat expansion, NRR of AI SKUs (DDOG/NOW-class disclosures). A renewal is the one signal an announcement cannot fake.
3. **Vertical trade press (NOT tech press):** pharma/legal/insurance/logistics industry publications — practitioner-reported usage. Origin of the pharma-vertical N≥3 triangulation (2026-07-01 artifact).
4. **Labor telemetry:** postings shifting pilot→operations/maintenance roles; filing-attributed headcount changes. Production systems need maintainers.
5. **Public procurement (T1 by construction):** SAM.gov, EU TED — signed contracts with deliverables.
6. **Usage telemetry proxies:** API-traffic/model-usage rankings, cloud-marketplace best-sellers, token-volume reports. Tokens, not narratives.
7. **Friction signals inverted:** lawsuits/works-council disputes/compliance rulings about AI use = deployment at scale proven adversarially (M365 Copilot class-action = this class, in corpus 2026-07-06). Nobody sues over a pilot.
8. **Institutionalization layer:** AI-liability insurance pricing, audit-firm AI-controls frameworks — permanent scaffolding signals.
