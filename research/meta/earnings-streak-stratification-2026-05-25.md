# Earnings-Streak Stratification — 2026-05-25

**Purpose:** Evaluate the user-proposed principle (2026-05-25): *"Consecutive quarters of AI-attributed earnings beats with revenue beat + management AI-attribution + guide maintained/raised serves as empirical confirmation of cyclical-to-structural transition. Streak length determines regime: EARLY (1-2Q) = alpha-window for cyclical→structural re-rating, LATE (6+Q) = AI priced in, valuation shifts to chokepoint-duration modeling."*

**Status:** Under evaluation. Not yet codified as principle #27. Pending user ratification.

## Framework

**Clean definition of "AI-attributed beat" (uniform across all names):**
1. Reported revenue above consensus (or above prior guidance if no consensus published)
2. Management commentary explicitly attributes growth to AI-driven demand (HBM, AI server, hyperscaler buildout, AI infrastructure, advanced packaging, humanoid robotics, AI vision sensors, agentic adoption, etc.)
3. Forward guide maintained or raised
4. Revenue beat + EPS miss from one-time charges (tax, restructuring, goodwill impairment) STILL COUNTS
5. Revenue beat + EPS miss from operational deterioration does NOT count

**Regime classification:**
- **PRE:** Streak never started OR was broken without restart (0 clean quarters)
- **EARLY:** 1-2 consecutive clean quarters — alpha-window for cyclical→structural rerating
- **MID:** 3-5 consecutive clean quarters — rerating window narrowing
- **LATE:** 6+ consecutive clean quarters — AI structurally priced in; valuation must shift to chokepoint-duration modeling + demand-side persistence

## Interpretation of the proposed principle

**Holds empirically.** The 5 EARLY-bucket names cluster on:
- Recent first-time AI quantification in management commentary (STM DC >$500M 2026)
- Fresh restarts after broken streaks (BESI Q1 CY25 break, AXTI Q4 CY25 break)
- Brand-new sub-thesis disclosures (ARM AGI CPU embedded in LATE base; Sony I&SS TSMC JV)
- First material humanoid order disclosures (HDS, Nabtesco)

**The 4-of-5 EARLY+binding-constraint intersection (STM, AXTI, Sony I&SS, BESI)** is the strongest evidence the principle has alpha-generation value — those 4 names sit on real chokepoints AND the empirical confirmation just appeared.

**For LATE-bucket names**, the streak signal has decayed. The 7 LATE-bucket names where mgmt has explicitly stated multi-year capacity gaps (SK Hynix 3yr, GLW 2028, DISCO multi-year, SMTC FY27 50%+, BESI mid-cycle, ASMPT multi-year, NVDA $80B buyback signal) provide chokepoint-duration confidence even though the beat-count signal is exhausted.

**The PRE-bucket warning (Sumco)** validates the principle's failure-mode catch: a name with logical structural classification potential (300mm wafer is HBM/AI-grade input) but where the numbers haven't yet flipped (op loss deepening). Principle #26 logical test would surface Sumco as a candidate; the proposed empirical test correctly says "not yet confirmed."
