# Causal Map — Power Becomes Binding

**Last updated:** 2026-05-20
**Trigger:** Hyperscaler capex growth (+77% to $725B in 2026) outpacing US/global firm-power capacity addition

## TL;DR

If power siting becomes the binding constraint in 2026 H2 / 2027 H1, the trade is: (a) firm-power producers re-price up, (b) hyperscaler compute deploy rates cap, (c) chip efficiency premium widens. This map is HIGH-LEVERAGE because it's partially priced — VST/CEG already up, but more leg if it actually binds.

## 1st order (P > 80% conditional on the trigger)

- **New datacenter sites without firm power get delayed.** Grid interconnect queues run 2–7 years in most ISO regions (MISO, PJM, ERCOT).
- **Existing sites' utilization gets pushed harder** — more value extracted per watt.
- **Power prices in datacenter regions rise.** Hyperscaler PPAs already crossing $50/MWh and rising.
- **Behind-the-meter + co-located generation accelerates.** Nuclear restart deals (Three Mile Island / Constellation-Microsoft archetype) multiply.

**Direct beneficiaries:** VST (gas + nuclear + retail), CEG (nuclear pure-play), TLN (nuclear+gas), GEV (turbines for new gas), NEE (regulated utility with large interconnect pipeline)

## 2nd order (P ~60%)

### Chip efficiency premium widens
- Performance-per-watt becomes the dominant chip-buying criterion (not just performance)
- Blackwell GB300 efficiency advantage vs prior gens becomes a deeper moat
- Custom inference silicon (TPU, Trainium) gains because they're more watt-efficient for specific workloads
- **Beneficiaries:** NVDA Blackwell/Rubin efficiency story, GOOG TPU, AMZN Trainium

### Liquid cooling becomes universal at density
- Power constraint forces datacenters to maximize watts/sqft → higher density → mandatory liquid cooling
- GB300 NVL72 already ships with ~$50K liquid cooling per rack
- **Beneficiaries:** VRT, specialized cooling vendors, OEMs equipped for liquid (SMCI, DELL liquid systems)

### Electrical equipment supply tightens
- Transformer lead times already extending past 24 months
- Switchgear, busbars, MV/LV electrical all in demand
- **Beneficiaries:** ETN (electrical), GEV (heavy electrical), Hubbell, Hammond Power

### Regional bifurcation of AI deployment
- Texas, Virginia, Arizona, Ohio with power capacity stay attractive
- California, parts of Europe, water-constrained regions lose deployments
- **Beneficiaries:** REITs in firm-power regions; **casualties:** datacenter REITs without power growth

## 3rd order (P ~40%)

### Hyperscaler revenue growth caps below capex implied
- If they can't deploy chips on schedule, AI services revenue can't grow as fast as guided
- The "AI revenue / AI capex" ratio gets watched harder
- Stocks re-rate down on perceived ROI risk
- **Casualties:** hyperscalers with thin AI-revenue stories (META the strongest defense via Reels/ads ROI)

### M&A in independent power producers
- Larger utilities + private equity bid for VST/CEG/TLN type assets
- Nuclear assets specifically become trophy assets
- **Beneficiaries:** existing IPP holders (potential takeout premium)

### Sovereign capital flows into power+AI bundle
- UAE/Saudi/Gulf sovereign deals package power + AI infra together (already happening — Stargate, UAE)
- Creates non-hyperscaler demand channel
- **Beneficiaries:** NVDA (direct sovereign deals), Bechtel (private — datacenter buildouts), GEV (electrical export)

### NVDA's full-stack efficiency narrative gets amplified
- "Buy our rack and you get more inference per watt than building your own" becomes Jensen's main pitch
- GB300 NVL72 + Spectrum-X integration justifies premium even more
- **Beneficiaries:** NVDA premium pricing power persists despite custom-Si pressure

## 4th order (P ~20%)

### Edge inference accelerates (latency + power both)
- Power-constrained core datacenters push some workloads to edge
- Edge silicon (ARM, custom NPUs) gets investment

### Modular nuclear + SMRs become real assets (not just promises)
- 5–10 year horizon, but inflection in financing happens within 2027
- **Beneficiaries:** GEV (their BWRX-300 SMR), CEG (deployment partner), private SMR cos

### AI-power ESG bifurcation
- Some allocators de-risk via "AI-positive, fossil-light" mandates
- Nuclear becomes the politically clean firm-power answer
- **Beneficiaries:** CEG specifically (pure nuclear)

## Signals confirming this map plays

1. New hyperscaler PPAs at >$70/MWh (signal: power scarcity)
2. ISO interconnect queue extensions beyond current 5–7 years
3. New nuclear restart announcements (any of Three Mile Island, Palisades, etc.)
4. Transformer lead times reported >30 months
5. Hyperscaler reporting "delayed datacenter deployments due to power" in earnings calls
6. CEG / VST share price decoupling further from broader utility index

## Signals invalidating this map

1. Hyperscaler capex pause (S4 — power demand softens)
2. Major regulatory permit reform speeds new generation (lower probability but possible)
3. Inference workloads transition heavily to edge/embodied (reduces datacenter load growth)
