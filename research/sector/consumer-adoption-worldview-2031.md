## §1 The method (fitted 1999, out-of-sample-checked 2007; N=2 convergence)
Both cycles independently produced the same core; unified algorithm ("PCPL-T"):
1. **GENERATE from primitives, never artifacts** — enumerate what the platform newly makes free/possible (1999: distribution; 2008: camera/GPS/graph/push/payments; the launch-day apps are always the wrong list).
2. **DEMAND GATE — behavioral precedent:** the VERB must already exist in analog form, friction-gated (socialize/watch/shop/delegate). Net-new behavior with no analog → reject (Color, check-in, QR-West).
2b. **FRICTION-DELTA GATE (added v2, adversarial amendment — the gate that rejects the metaverse and Alexa):** the new form must NET-REDUCE total user friction vs the dominant analog, after counting the friction the new primitive itself introduces. Verb-precedent with a friction INCREASE = REJECT (VR-social: headset/nausea/isolation; voice-commerce: can't see the product). Backtest: with this gate, the method now REJECTS the two known consumer-capital incinerations it previously fit right past — metaverse 2016-23 (Reality Labs ~$80-84B cumulative, Horizon ~200k MAU at discontinuation) and Alexa-commerce 2014-18 (devices >$25B losses 2017-21, delegation-verb failed).
3. **FORM GATE — primitive-novelty:** the FORM must exploit the new primitive, not port an old artifact (portals/iTV/WAP/mobile-Flash all died as ports). Verb-precedent + form-novelty TOGETHER — this composition is what both retrodictions converged on.
4. **ASSET GATE — compounding demand-side asset:** user N must improve the product for user N+1 (index/graph/corpus/purchase-log) or deepen per-user switching cost. This is the Amazon-vs-pets.com and Google-vs-Yahoo discriminator.
5. **REJECT:** fights the platform owner; cold-start with no bootstrap graph; enabling primitive not at consumer scale → not reject but **WATCHLIST + explicit trigger** (the highest-alpha instruction: 1999's correct video call was "wait for broadband ~20-30% penetration," and YouTube launched exactly on that crossover).
6. **TIMING LAW (both cycles):** infra inflection → wave-1 winners founded +1-3yr, at-scale +4-7yr; **wave-2 winners need primitives that don't exist at platform launch** (TikTok's front-cam/LTE/feed-ML) → platform-to-winner 9-11yr; later-founded winners scale FASTER once their primitive crosses. Expert-failure direction, both cycles: debating share of the current artifact (portal wars, smartphone-share wars) while the primitives sat in plain sight.
**VALIDATION RELABEL (v2, mandatory adversarial amendment): "N=2 convergence" was selection on the dependent variable — both reference cycles were chosen BECAUSE they produced Facebook-class winners; the method is "N=2 FIT, validated only against the disconfirming cycles via the v2 friction-delta backtest above." The waypoints, not the fit, decide.**
**Contamination (inherited, binding):** the asset-gate's increasing-returns qualifier and the good-burn/bad-burn discriminator carry the most hindsight; the loser-gates are the cleanest ex-ante pieces. This method is a LENS with N=2 historical fit, applied out-of-sample below — the waypoints, not the lens, decide if it works.

## §3 The lattice (~2031; branches non-exclusive — they compete for the cycle's Facebook slot; P = my model)
- **S1 CONTEXT WINS (P~30, v2-reweighted):** the compounding context-vault becomes the decade's demand-side asset. Interface: one persistent entity across every surface (phone/glasses/car) that knows you completely; "switching AI" becomes as unthinkable as abandoning your Google account in 2010. Winner: a lab converting assistant-reach into context-moat (OpenAI best-positioned, T1 memory data) OR Apple (device-context) OR the neutral-vault entrant incumbents can't build (Facebook-shaped hole #1). At-scale ≈ 2029-31 (wave-1 lag math).
- **S2 AGENCY WINS (P~15, v2-reweighted down on the Alexa disconfirmer):** trust rails mature 2027-28 (Visa/MC agent-rails T1 + dispute-rights legislation = the "push notification 2009" moment) → by 2031 a meaningful share of consumer transactions are agent-executed; the winner is the FIDUCIARY (represents the user against merchants — cannibalizes ad+interchange economics, hence a new entrant). The physical-world (Uber-class 5yr) lag applies: at-scale ≈ 2030-33.
- **S3 ATTENTION MIGRATES (P~15, v2-reweighted):** the engagement branch — compliance-native companions + per-person generated media/worlds capture the attention economy (embryo: TikTok already ~52-59% AI-involved content T2; companions 4-8x assistant engagement). The 2031 feed is majority per-person-generated; the "Roblox of world models" launches on the compute-cost crossover ≈ 2028-29, scales fast (YouTube-law: late winners scale faster).
- **S4 THE UNNAMEABLE WINNER (P~40, v2 — mandatory adversarial amendment):** the biggest winner of this cycle sits on a primitive not yet at consumer scale or not yet conceived — the TikTok/Facebook slot. Base rate from the retrodictions: 0/2 cycles had their biggest winner nameable at t+3.6yr. S4 is not a hedge; it is the method's own central finding given its correct weight. Operational content: keep the embryo screen running every wake; the S4 winner announces itself as an embryo with anomalous organic retention on a primitive the share-war discourse ignores.
**EXCLUDED-STATES BLOCK (v2): two world-states deliberately weighted OUTSIDE the lattice** (they answer "does a Facebook happen at all," not "which primitive"): (i) **CONSUMER-AI WINTER** — engagement plateaus (current evidence BOTH ways: ChatGPT D30 retention ~4% T3 vs billion-MAU reach; C.AI shrinking); P~15 (my model) there is NO Facebook-class consumer winner by 2031 — utility-without-love, all value accrues to infra + enterprise.
