## §1 The method (fitted 1999, out-of-sample-checked 2007; N=2 convergence)
Both cycles independently produced the same core; unified algorithm ("PCPL-T"):
1. **GENERATE from primitives, never artifacts** — enumerate what the platform newly makes free/possible (1999: distribution; 2008: camera/GPS/graph/push/payments; the launch-day apps are always the wrong list).
2. **DEMAND GATE — behavioral precedent:** the VERB must already exist in analog form, friction-gated (socialize/watch/shop/delegate). Net-new behavior with no analog → reject (Color, check-in, QR-West).
3. **FORM GATE — primitive-novelty:** the FORM must exploit the new primitive, not port an old artifact (portals/iTV/WAP/mobile-Flash all died as ports). Verb-precedent + form-novelty TOGETHER — this composition is what both retrodictions converged on.
4. **ASSET GATE — compounding demand-side asset:** user N must improve the product for user N+1 (index/graph/corpus/purchase-log) or deepen per-user switching cost. This is the Amazon-vs-pets.com and Google-vs-Yahoo discriminator.
5. **REJECT:** fights the platform owner; cold-start with no bootstrap graph; enabling primitive not at consumer scale → not reject but **WATCHLIST + explicit trigger** (the highest-alpha instruction: 1999's correct video call was "wait for broadband ~20-30% penetration," and YouTube launched exactly on that crossover).
6. **TIMING LAW (both cycles):** infra inflection → wave-1 winners founded +1-3yr, at-scale +4-7yr; **wave-2 winners need primitives that don't exist at platform launch** (TikTok's front-cam/LTE/feed-ML) → platform-to-winner 9-11yr; later-founded winners scale FASTER once their primitive crosses. Expert-failure direction, both cycles: debating share of the current artifact (portal wars, smartphone-share wars) while the primitives sat in plain sight.
**Contamination (inherited, binding):** the asset-gate's increasing-returns qualifier and the good-burn/bad-burn discriminator carry the most hindsight; the loser-gates are the cleanest ex-ante pieces. This method is a LENS with N=2 historical fit, applied out-of-sample below — the waypoints, not the lens, decide if it works.

## §2 Applied to AI (inflection = ChatGPT Nov-2022; today = t+3.6yr ≈ 1999-in-internet-time / 2011-in-mobile-time)
**What AI makes ~free (measured, per leg 4):** cognition (~10-50x/yr cost decline), 1:1 personalization (tutoring 80-95% cheaper — the luxury-good cost structure that collapsed), software creation (per-person disposable apps), media generation (2-4 orders of magnitude). **The two load-bearing collapses = personalization + software creation** (this cycle's "free distribution").
**Today's expert-failure direction (the method predicts it):** the discourse is model-share wars (OpenAI vs Google vs Anthropic benchmark races) — the exact analog of portal wars and smartphone-share wars. The primitives sit in plain sight: persistent context, delegated agency, synthetic relationship, generative worlds, disposable software.

## §3 The lattice (~2031; branches non-exclusive — they compete for the cycle's Facebook slot; P = my model)
- **S1 CONTEXT WINS (P~40):** the compounding context-vault becomes the decade's demand-side asset. Interface: one persistent entity across every surface (phone/glasses/car) that knows you completely; "switching AI" becomes as unthinkable as abandoning your Google account in 2010. Winner: a lab converting assistant-reach into context-moat (OpenAI best-positioned, T1 memory data) OR Apple (device-context) OR the neutral-vault entrant incumbents can't build (Facebook-shaped hole #1). At-scale ≈ 2029-31 (wave-1 lag math).
- **S2 AGENCY WINS (P~30):** trust rails mature 2027-28 (Visa/MC agent-rails T1 + dispute-rights legislation = the "push notification 2009" moment) → by 2031 a meaningful share of consumer transactions are agent-executed; the winner is the FIDUCIARY (represents the user against merchants — cannibalizes ad+interchange economics, hence a new entrant). The physical-world (Uber-class 5yr) lag applies: at-scale ≈ 2030-33.
- **S3 ATTENTION MIGRATES (P~30):** the engagement branch — compliance-native companions + per-person generated media/worlds capture the attention economy (embryo: TikTok already ~52-59% AI-involved content T2; companions 4-8x assistant engagement). The 2031 feed is majority per-person-generated; the "Roblox of world models" launches on the compute-cost crossover ≈ 2028-29, scales fast (YouTube-law: late winners scale faster).
**Cross-branch invariant (decision-relevant):** ALL THREE branches are inference-demand-compounding — context retrieval, agent execution loops, and world generation are each more compute-per-user than chat. The consumer worldview REINFORCES the infra book in every branch (ties to macro: TC-1 demand mechanism, KV-cache/Patel; the funding-shock node governs the pace, not the direction).

## §4 Surprise quota (mechanism-backed absurdities, one per branch — the "upload your whole life" class)
1. (S1) **By ~2031 the modal person's primary confidant — the entity that knows them best, incl. health — is an AI**, and this is as unremarkable as uploading photos became. Mechanism: health residue already 1-in-6 adults INSIDE ChatGPT (T1) + companion engagement + context compounding. The productized version (compliance-native AI clinician/counsel) is a top-5 consumer app of 2031.
2. (S2) **Consumers stop seeing prices**: your agent negotiates per-transaction; posted-price retail becomes the minority of consumer commerce. Mechanism: fiduciary economics + agent-rails; precedent = nobody sees airfare "real" prices now.
3. (S3) **Per-person media exceeds shared media**: the majority of a heavy user's feed exists only for them (generated), collapsing "did you see X" culture. Mechanism: TikTok AI-share curve + generation cost collapse; precedent = the algorithmic-feed personalization ramp 2016-22.
